Character Group Pages

Snowball

Group: Sophia F, Caio, Miguel

This is the dedicated space for the **Snowball** group's project. When the final pages are complete, this section will be replaced with their HTML content.

Mock Content Template:

Snowball represents the idealistic but ultimately exiled intellectual, often compared to Leon Trotsky. His focus on expanding literacy and planning the windmill demonstrated a genuine commitment to improving life on the farm, a vision tragically undermined by Napoleon's opportunism.

Napoleon

Group: Sophia O, Rafaella, Geraldo, Leo

This is the dedicated space for the **Napoleon** group's project.

Mock Content Template:

Napoleon embodies the ruthless, totalitarian dictator, mirroring Joseph Stalin. His ascent is marked by manipulation, reliance on the brute force of the dogs, and the gradual erosion of the initial revolutionary principles. He prioritizes personal power over the welfare of the animals.

Squealer

Group: MaLu, Isabella Y, Gui

This is the dedicated space for the **Squealer** group's project.

Mock Content Template:

Squealer is the master propagandist, using convoluted language, statistics, and fear tactics to justify Napoleon's every action. He represents the state-controlled media and propaganda machine that keeps the populace compliant and misinformed.

Boxer

Group: Sofia G, Isabela S, Ryan

This is the dedicated space for the **Boxer** group's project.

Mock Content Template:

Boxer is the dedicated, loyal, and incredibly strong working class. His slogans, "I will work harder" and "Napoleon is always right," highlight his noble yet naive devotion to the cause, which is ultimately betrayed by the pig leadership when he is no longer useful.

Moses & Mollie

Group: Manuela P, Julia X, Enrico

This is the dedicated space for the **Moses & Mollie** group's project.

Mock Content Template:

Moses, the raven, symbolizes religion ("Sugarcandy Mountain"), used to distract the working class from their hardships. Mollie, the white mare, represents the frivolous upper class who quickly flee the revolution because they prioritize luxuries over ideology.

Clover & Benjamin

Group: Sophia T, Samantha, Joca

This is the dedicated space for the **Clover & Benjamin** group's project.

Mock Content Template:

Clover represents the maternal, questioning part of the working class who suspects the pigs are breaking the Commandments but lacks the intellectual capacity to act. Benjamin, the cynical donkey, represents the intelligentsia who understand the truth but refuse to intervene, believing revolution will always fail.

The Humans

Group: Manuela R, Mirela, Gaga, Peçanha

This is the dedicated space for **The Humans** group's project.

Mock Content Template:

The humans—Mr. Jones, Mr. Pilkington, and Mr. Frederick—represent the decadent Tsar Nicholas II and the capitalist Western powers. They initially fear the revolution but ultimately find ways to exploit the animals, culminating in the chilling final scene where the pigs become indistinguishable from their human counterparts.
